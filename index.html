Progate Animals and Plants is an animal and plant lover organization that integrates wildlife health and care, science, and education to develop sustainable conservation solutions. Conservation is at the heart of everything we do. And it starts with the relationships we make with humans and wildlife every day. Because when wildlife thrives, all life thrives.

ARTHROPODS

Arthropods is a phylum that includes insects and spiders. They are invertebrates, which means they do not have an internal skeleton and backbone..

MAMMALS

Mammals are vertebrate animals that are endothermic, have hair on their bodies, and produce milk to feed their babies.

FISH

Fish live in just about all aquatic habitats on Earth, from deep oceans to trickling mountain brooks.

REPTILES

Reptiles are vertebrates, they have scaly skin that keeps their bodies from drying out, and their young do not go through a larva stage like amphibians.

BIRD

Birds are vertebrates, with a backbone and skeleton, although some of the bones are hollow to keep the bird light and aloft.

AMPHIBIANS

Amphibians live part of their lives in water and part on land. They are vertebrates and are also ectothermic; they cannot regulate their own body heat, so they depend on sunlight to become warm and active.
